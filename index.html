Intro

Hello, My name is Fahim Zaman. I have a passion for creation, technology, and problem-solving. I am a computer science student who recently transferred from Los Angeles Pierce College to attend the Univerity of California, Santa Barbara.

I think the world of computer sciences holds the key to our future especially with AI and I am eager to contribute towards it as much as I can. I will be having a growing list of projects in my Portfolio so feel to check that out.

In addition to my projects, you can also view the About section to find more details about my professional journey, skills, and achievements.

If you are interested in reading my thoughts on different computer science topics, head over to the Blog section and if you have any question or would like to get in touch with me, go to Contact.

Thank you for visiting my website!
